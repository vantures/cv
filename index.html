Hi, I'm Carey

I have an affinity for creative automation, untangling messy workflows, and side projects that somehow turn into full-blown rabbit holes. Efficiency is my love language.

Projects

PassPath.ai
PassPath.ai is a CPA exam study site I built using Cursor, Lovable, Supabase, and OpenAI. I learned how to manage a large database, a live product, improve UX, and balance automation with accuracy.
Lunaby.ai
Lunaby is a bedtime story app I built using Supabase, Lovable, and OpenAI. It generates personalized stories and images based on a child’s name and interests, with audio narration via ElevenLabs. I learned how to build engaging user flows and balance creativity with performance.
Pecking Order
Pecking Order is a Wingspan-inspired web game I built using Cursor, Supabase, and OpenAI. It helps players decide who goes first in board games through a bird-themed race. I used it to explore game logic, playful design, and how to keep things lightweight and fun on both desktop and mobile.

About

I'm a CPA with over 15 years of experience, including 8 years focused on revenue accounting in the fast-paced world of SaaS. I like making things work better, whether that means automating a clunky workflow, building internal tools, or eliminating one more pointless mouse click. Outside of work, I’m a husband, a dad of two, and someone with probably too many hobbies. I’m usually reading something interesting, chasing live music, or brainstorming new ideas. I believe good work comes from a mix of curiosity, technical skill, and a healthy desire to keep improving things, even just 1% at a time.
